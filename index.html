Frequently Asked Questions

What types of sales data files can I upload?

SalesDash currently supports CSV (.csv) and Excel (.xlsx) file formats. Simply drag and drop your file or use the "Browse Files" button on the home page.

Is my sales data secure and private?

Yes, we prioritize the security and privacy of your data. All uploaded data is processed securely and is only accessible by you. We do not share or store your sensitive sales information permanently without explicit consent (e.g., if you opt for a plan with cloud storage).

Can I customize the dashboard's appearance?

Absolutely! You can customize the dashboard's theme and colors via the "Settings" page. We offer several preset color options to match your preference.

What if my CSV columns don't match the expected fields?

Our intelligent auto-mapping feature will attempt to identify your columns. If needed, you'll be guided through a simple mapping process where you can manually select which of your columns correspond to our required fields (e.g., "Total Sale," "Sold Unit"). You can re-map columns anytime from the Settings page.

Can I download or share my generated dashboard?

Yes, SalesDash allows you to download your dashboard as a PDF or individual image files. Depending on your plan, you may also have options to generate private shareable links to your dashboard.

Still have questions?

If you cannot find an answer to your question in our FAQ, you can always contact us directly. We will answer you shortly!
